Loom Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Insert date]

This Privacy Policy describes how Loom collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you use the platform. By accessing or using Loom, you consent to these practices. If you share information about clients or team members, you represent that you have the legal right to do so and that you have provided any notices or obtained any consents required under applicable law.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and authentication data: email, username, role (coach, client, admin), password hashes, email verification and password reset tokens, and language preferences.
  • Profile and relationship data: coach and client profiles, invitations, client types (invited, offline, registered), avatars, and coach-client assignments.
  • Somatic and session data: body zones, sensations, intensity levels, reflections, session history, recurring schedules, goals, milestones, analytics snapshots, and exports generated for reporting.
  • Scheduling and availability: availability slots, booking status, held slots, and calendar connections.
  • Files and resources: uploaded files (e.g., resources, session attachments, avatars), associated storage keys, download URLs, and resource library entries.
  • Payments and subscriptions: plan selections, transaction identifiers, tokens for renewal attempts, subscription status, retry tracking, customer portal links, and payment processor identifiers.
  • Communications: contact form messages, admin correspondence, notifications, and email sending metadata.
  • Usage and analytics: cookie preferences, analytics events, logs, and page-view sources collected through services such as Plausible or Google Analytics (when enabled).

2. How We Use Information

  • To register and authenticate users, manage roles, and enable onboarding and invitations.
  • To provide coach and client features such as somatic logging, session tracking, goals, scheduling, availability, exports, and analytics dashboards.
  • To process payments, manage subscriptions, handle retries, and provide access to customer portals via third-party processors.
  • To store and deliver files and shared resources you upload.
  • To send service communications (e.g., verification, password reset, invitations, notifications) and respond to contact messages.
  • To improve reliability, monitor usage, detect misuse, and develop new features through aggregated analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Loom uses a consent banner to manage cookies. Necessary cookies support core functionality. When enabled and accepted, analytics cookies (e.g., Plausible, Google Analytics) collect usage information to help us improve the service. You can change your preferences through the cookie controls available in the banner.

4. How We Share Information

  • With coaches and invited collaborators: Client data, somatic logs, goals, schedules, files, and analytics are shared with the coach responsible for that client.
  • Service providers: Hosting and storage providers, email delivery services (e.g., SendGrid), payment processors (e.g., Tranzilla, Stripe, Lemon Squeezy), analytics providers (e.g., Plausible, Google Analytics), and similar vendors assisting in operating the platform.
  • Legal and safety: To comply with law, respond to legal requests, or protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, in accordance with applicable laws.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, fulfill legitimate business purposes (including backups, audits, and security), comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Account data, somatic logs, sessions, files, analytics, and transaction records may be retained for these purposes unless deletion is requested and permitted by law.

6. Security

We employ technical and organizational measures designed to protect information, including role-based access controls, secure payment processing through third-party providers, and monitoring jobs that help detect anomalies. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your credentials and devices.

7. International Data Transfers

Depending on your location and the locations of our service providers, information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required by law.

8. Your Choices & Rights

  • You may update account details, language preferences, and notification settings within the app.
  • You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of personal information, subject to authentication and legal limitations.
  • You can manage cookie preferences through the consent banner.
  • To unsubscribe from non-transactional emails, use provided opt-out mechanisms or contact us.

9. Children's Privacy

The service is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. Do not use Loom if you are under 18 or if prohibited by applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated when required. Continued use after changes become effective signifies acceptance of the updated Policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact loomcoachingplatform@gmail.com or use the in-app contact options.
